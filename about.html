About the National Cadet Corps

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth development movement that aims to empower young individuals with skills in leadership, discipline, and national pride. It is a voluntary organization that encourages students to join and engage in various community service and adventurous activities.

Key Activities and Training

  • Military Training
  • Community Service
  • Adventure Sports
  • Leadership Development

Join the NCC

Through NCC, students develop qualities like courage, confidence, and a sense of responsibility. They become well-rounded individuals ready to contribute positively to society.
